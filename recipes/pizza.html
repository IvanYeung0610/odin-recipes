Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is the perfect balance of simplicity and flavor, making it a great option for both weeknight dinners and weekend gatherings. The crust bakes up golden and crisp, while the melted mozzarella and savory toppings come together for a comforting and customizable dish. Whether you like your pizza classic with just cheese and pepperoni or loaded with veggies and herbs, this recipe serves as a reliable base. It’s also a fun, hands-on cooking experience—especially if you’re making it with family or friends. Once you try making pizza at home, you might never go back to takeout!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded low moisture mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, basil, etc...)
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  2. Prepare your workspace by lightly flouring a clean surface.
  3. Roll or stretch the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  4. Transfer the dough onto a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about ½ inch around the edges for the crust.
  6. Add shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  7. Top the pizza with your choice of ingredients (pepperoni, veggies, herbs, etc.).
  8. Carefully slide the pizza (with parchment paper) onto the hot pizza stone or baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and browned.
  10. Remove from the oven, let cool for a couple of minutes, then slice and serve.
