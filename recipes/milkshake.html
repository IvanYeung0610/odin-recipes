Milkshake

Description

This classic milkshake recipe is rich, creamy, and irresistibly smooth—perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth on a warm day or adding a nostalgic touch to dessert time. Made with just a few simple ingredients—ice cream, milk, and your choice of flavorings—it blends together in minutes for a thick and frosty treat. Whether you stick with a traditional vanilla or chocolate base or get creative with mix-ins like cookies, fruit, or syrups, this milkshake is easy to customize and even easier to enjoy. Serve it with a straw, a spoon, and maybe a cherry on top for that perfect diner-style finish.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla ice cream (or your preferred flavor)
  • Whole milk
  • Flavored syrup (chocolate, strawberry, caramel, etc...)
  • Whipped cream
  • Maraschino cherry (optional)
  • Sprinkles or crushed cookies (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop the ice cream into a blender. Use about 2 cups of your favorite flavor for a thick and creamy texture.
  2. Pour in the milk, starting with ½ cup. You can add more later if you prefer a thinner shake.
  3. Add flavored syrup if desired (such as chocolate, strawberry, or caramel) for extra flavor.
  4. Blend the mixture on high speed until smooth and creamy, about 30–60 seconds. Stop and scrape down the sides if needed.
  5. Check the consistency and add a splash more milk if it’s too thick, then blend again briefly.
  6. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  7. Top with whipped cream, a maraschino cherry, and sprinkles or crushed cookies if desired.
  8. Serve immediately with a straw and spoon, and enjoy!
